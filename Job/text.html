Job Application is the process through which a person applies for employment in an organization or company. In the modern digital era, many organizations use online job portals to receive applications from candidates. Applicants usually create a profile, upload their resume, and provide personal as well as educational details. These platforms help employers review applications quickly and select suitable candidates for interviews. Traditional methods such as paper-based job applications are now less commonly used because online systems are faster and more efficient.

A job application system often includes features like profile creation, job search filters, and interview notifications. Candidates can apply for multiple positions based on their skills and qualifications. Some platforms also provide career guidance and allow applicants to track their application status. In many job portals, different versions or levels such as Job Portal2 or Job Portal3 may include additionalfeatures. Applicants aim to improve their chances of selection by achieving high performance scores like102 in online assessments conducted by companies.
